Elyse Thulin, Behavioral & Applied Computational Scientist

I am an Assistant Research Professor at the University of Michigan.



I have several active areas of work:

1. Unpacking how anonymous/confidential reporting systems function and are used by youth.
2. Adolescent and emerging adult cyber violence exposure (e.g., cyber dating violence).
3. Mental health, relationships and substances use in adolescents and emerging adults.
