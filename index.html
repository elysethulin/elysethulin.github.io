Elyse Thulin, Social Scientist and Outdoor Enthusiast

I'm a doctoral student at the University of Michigan, studying behavioral and computational science.

I have several active areas of work:
Dating Violence in U.S. Youth Samples
Examining Intimate Partner Violence Across the Socioecological Model
Computational Science Applications in Reproductive Justice: Historical Analysis of 20th Century Sterilization in the United States
Sexual and Gender Based Violence in South Africa and the Democratic Republic of Congo
